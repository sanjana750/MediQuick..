About MediQuick

MediQuick is a platform connecting patients with qualified healthcare professionals for home visit services. Our mission is to provide convenient, compassionate, and professional healthcare for those who prefer or need treatment at home — including the elderly, post-surgery patients, and people with mobility challenges.

How It Works

  1. Contact MediQuick via phone or email and describe your healthcare needs.
  2. Our team matches you with the appropriate healthcare professional — doctors, nurses, physiotherapists, or lab technicians.
  3. The healthcare professional schedules a home visit at your convenience.
  4. You receive quality care right at your doorstep.

Our Services

  • Doctor Visits: Certified doctors for consultations, check-ups, and post-operative care.
  • Nursing Care: Skilled nurses for injections, wound dressing, IV therapy, and patient monitoring.
  • Physiotherapy: Rehabilitation and pain management exercises conducted at home.
  • Lab Tests: Sample collection by certified technicians with digital reports.

Patient Information & History

We maintain a secure record of your medical history and previous visits to provide personalized and continuous care. Your information is confidential and used only to improve your healthcare experience.
